About Eaze Zone

Rustomjee Eaze Zone Mall – where business meets opportunity, and shopping meets sophistication. A thriving hub in Malad West, it blends premium retail, corporate spaces, and modern amenities, ensuring success, convenience, and an unmatched experience under one roof.

About Us

Rustomjee Eaze Zone Mall is more than just a commercial space—it is a dynamic hub of business, retail, and lifestyle, thoughtfully designed to cater to modern entrepreneurs, brands, and shoppers alike. Nestled in the heart of Malad West, Mumbai, this premium commercial complex spans an impressive 1.21 acres, offering a perfect blend of functionality, elegance, and accessibility. With 618 meticulously designed commercial units, the mall is a thriving ecosystem that supports businesses of all scales, from high-end retail stores and boutique outlets to corporate offices and investment opportunities. Strategically positioned along the bustling Goregaon-Mulund Link Road, Rustomjee Eaze Zone Mall enjoys seamless connectivity to Mumbai’s major transit routes, including the Western Express Highway and Swami Vivekanand Road. This prime location ensures high visibility and consistent footfall, making it an attractive destination for brands looking to establish a strong presence in one of the city's most vibrant commercial districts. Beyond its strategic positioning, the mall is equipped with a host of world-class amenities that redefine the commercial experience. High-speed elevators ensure smooth access across floors, while ample parking facilities provide hassle-free convenience for visitors and business owners alike. The mall is under 24/7 security surveillance, ensuring a safe and secure environment for all. The contemporary design and thoughtful infrastructure make it not just a shopping destination but a space where businesses flourish, collaborations thrive, and customers indulge in an unmatched retail experience. Rustomjee Eaze Zone Mall is not just a commercial project; it is a vision for the future of business and commerce. Whether you are an entrepreneur seeking the perfect retail or office space, an investor looking for high-value opportunities, or a shopper searching for a refined and seamless experience, this mall offers everything under one roof. It is a place where innovation meets commerce, where success stories are written every day, and where the future of retail and business finds its home.
